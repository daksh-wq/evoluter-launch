### Question 1
**[TAGS]**
- **Target Exam:** UPSC CSE
- **Year:** 2024
- **Subject:** Economy
- **Topic:** Inflation
- **Difficulty:** Hard

**[QUESTION]**
Which of the following measures can be used to control inflation in an economy?
1. Increasing the repo rate.
2. Reducing government spending.
3. Increasing taxes.

**[OPTIONS]**
A. Only 1 and 2
B. Only 2 and 3
C. Only 1 and 3
D. 1, 2 and 3

**[CORRECT_ANSWER]**
3

**[EXPLANATION]**
Increasing the repo rate makes borrowing more expensive, reducing the money supply. Reducing government spending decreases aggregate demand. Increasing taxes reduces disposable income, also decreasing aggregate demand. All these measures help control inflation.
