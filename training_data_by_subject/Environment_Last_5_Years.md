## Topic: Biodiversity

### Question [2024]
**[TAGS]**
- **Target Exam:** UPSC CSE
- **Year:** 2024
- **Subject:** Environment
- **Topic:** Biodiversity
- **Difficulty:** Hard

**[QUESTION]**
Which of the following is/are examples of in-situ conservation?
1. National Parks
2. Botanical Gardens
3. Wildlife Sanctuaries

**[OPTIONS]**
A. Only 1
B. Only 2
C. Only 1 and 3
D. 1, 2 and 3

**[CORRECT_ANSWER]**
2

**[EXPLANATION]**
In-situ conservation involves conserving species within their natural habitats. National Parks and Wildlife Sanctuaries are examples of this. Botanical Gardens are ex-situ conservation, as they involve conserving species outside their natural habitats.

### Question [2021]

- **Target Exam:** UPSC CSE
- **Year:** 2021
- **Subject:** Environment
- **Topic:** Biodiversity
- **Difficulty:** Hard

**[QUESTION]**
Consider the following:
1. Bats
2. Bears
3. Rodents
The phenomenon of hibernation can be observed in which of the above?

**[OPTIONS]**
A. 1 and 2 only
B. 2 only
C. 1, 2 and 3
D. Hibernation cannot be observed in any of the above

**[CORRECT_ANSWER]**
1

**[EXPLANATION]**
Hibernation is a state of inactivity characterized by a decrease in body temperature, breathing rate, heart rate, and metabolic rate. Bears are well-known hibernators. While some bats and rodents enter a state of torpor, it is not considered true hibernation in the same way as bears.

Which of the following is/are the cause/causes of chemical contamination of water?
1. Excessive use of nitrogenous fertilizers
2. Use of detergents
3. Use of pesticides
Select the correct answer using the code given below:

**[OPTIONS]**
A. 1 only
B. 2 and 3 only
C. 1 and 3 only
D. 1, 2 and 3

**[CORRECT_ANSWER]**
